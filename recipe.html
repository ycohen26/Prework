Soggy Donuts Recipe

I hope you're as excited for this recipe as I am!!! This one totally takes me back to my childhood! Remember, back in the day, when you would leave your Dunkin' Donut sitting in your milk and then remember hours later that you left it there? Yuuuummmm. That's what this recipe will take you back to. Those scrumtious soggy donuts. This time though, you can enjoy it right away!!!!!

Ingredients

  • 10 Cups Water
  • 2 Cups flour
  • 5 Cups Sugar
  • 1 Cup Oil
  • 1 tbsp Baking Powder
  • A Pinch of Salt

Directions

  1. Mix flower and water until batter is super liquidy and won't really hold.
  2. Mix in sugar, salt and baking powder. Make sure it's distributed evenly.
  3. Place oil in a deep pot and heat on high until it starts bubbling.
  4. Do what you can to make the batter into a donut shape (this part is particulary difficult ebing that you have a sloppy mess right now.)
  5. Carefully place your blob into the oil until partially cooked.
  6. Remove the donut just as it starts to brown. Remeber, you don;t really want to let it cook to much. Keep it gross and soggy.
  7. Place on paper towels for a few minutes to cool.
  8. Enjoy your sloppy treat!
